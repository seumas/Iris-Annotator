About Iris Annotator

Iris Annotator is a high-performance, 100% local utility for adding permanent highlights, notes, and redaction-style markup to PDF documents en masse.

Because Iris processes everything directly in your browser's memory, your private manuscripts and sensitive documents never leave your computer.

Match Types Explained

  • Partial (abc): Matches text anywhere. "doc" highlights inside "document".
  • Exact (""): Matches whole words only. "doc" only matches the word "doc".
  • Regex (.*): Uses Regular Expressions for advanced pattern finding.
    Learn/Test Regex here
  • Case Sensitive (Aa): Forces exact casing (e.g. "Apple" will not match "apple").

Selective Stripping

Clicking "Strip Annotations" allows you to scan PDFs and permanently delete Highlights, Underlines, Strikeouts, and Sticky Notes. You can strip everything, or only remove annotations made by a specific Author or in a specific Color.

Note: This is non-destructive (it generates a new, clean copy) and applies to all queued files at once. If you only want to strip a specific document, queue only that file.

Workspace Profiles

Save your rules, author tags, and naming settings as Profiles. Switch between setups instantly, or export/import them as JSON files to back up your workflow or share with team members.
